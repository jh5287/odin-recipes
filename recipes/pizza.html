Homemade Pizza

Description

An easy homemade pizza to make when you have the right ingredients, a lot of time on your hands, and some friends to share it with!

Ingredients

Crust

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tabelspoon fast rise yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tabelspoon oil

Toppings

  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoom garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded monterey jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1: In large bowl, mix first four ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Mix water and oil, add to flour mixture.
  3. Step 3: Turn onto floured surface; knead for 2 minutes
  4. Step 4: Place in a greased bowl; turning to grease top.
  5. Step 5: Cover and let rise for 20 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Punch down; place on 12in greased pizza pan.
  7. Step 7: Pat into a circle.
  8. Step 8: Topping: mix first 5 ingredients and spread over crust.
  9. Step 9: Put a few pepperoni slices on top of sauce.
  10. Step 10: Sprinkle with 1/2 the mozzerella; 1/2 the monterey jack, and 1/2 the parmesan<./li>
  11. Step 11: Put the rest of the pepperoni on.
  12. Step 12: Repeat the cheese layer.
  13. Step 13: Bake at 400* for 20 minutes or until light brown.
